About fishing

Fishing has become an international sport that is common across all age ranges. Fishing has always been a system of hunting and gathering and has now even become a source of entertainment and an opportunity to explore God's beautiful creation. 

This site is based out of Greenville, South Carolina, home to some beautiful creeks and rivers. Greenville has some excellent fishing spots and there are probably some prominent areas near YOU! From oceans to ponds, fishing territory is everywhere all over the world. 

What to fish for

You can fish for all kinds of different fish, from freshwater brim to huge saltwater sharks. Freshwater fish are most prominently represented by some species of bass and brim. Saltwater fish are typically represented by red-fish and larger fish such as maji or even sharks.
